Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about furnace installation in Pasadena

How do I know if I need a new furnace?
Consider replacing your furnace if it's over 15 years old, requires frequent repairs, has significantly increased your energy bills, makes unusual noises, or doesn't heat your home evenly. Our technicians can perform an inspection to determine if repair or replacement is the better option.
How much does a new furnace installation cost in Pasadena?
New furnace installation typically ranges from $2,800 to $7,500 in Pasadena, depending on the system's efficiency rating, size, brand, and features. Standard efficiency models start around $2,800, mid-efficiency around $3,600, and high-efficiency systems start at $4,500. We provide detailed, transparent quotes after assessing your specific needs.
How long does furnace installation take?
A typical furnace installation takes about 4-8 hours, depending on the complexity of the installation and whether any modifications to existing ductwork are needed. Replacement installations are usually completed in one day, while new construction or system conversions may take 1-2 days.
What size furnace do I need for my home?
Furnace sizing depends on several factors including your home's square footage, insulation levels, window efficiency, ceiling height, and local climate. Our technicians perform a detailed load calculation following industry standards to determine the precise size needed for maximum efficiency and comfort. Improper sizing can lead to energy waste and premature system failure.
Are there any rebates or tax credits available for new furnaces?
Yes, energy-efficient furnaces may qualify for local utility rebates, manufacturer rebates, and federal tax credits. Currently, furnaces with 95% AFUE or higher may qualify for rebates of $150-$500 from local utilities and potential tax credits. We'll help you identify all available incentives to maximize your savings.
What maintenance is required for a new furnace?
New furnaces require annual professional maintenance to maintain efficiency, extend equipment life, and keep warranties valid. This includes inspection, cleaning, and tune-up of components. Homeowners should change air filters every 1-3 months depending on usage and air quality. We offer maintenance plans that include priority service and discounts on repairs.
