HVAC Tips for Pasadena Homeowners

Expert advice tailored to Pasadena's unique climate challenges

Surviving Pasadena Heat Waves

When temperatures soar into the 90s during Pasadena summers, your AC works overtime. Pre-cool your home in the morning, use ceiling fans, close blinds on south-facing windows, and schedule annual AC maintenance before summer arrives.

Managing Wildfire Season Air Quality

During Pasadena's wildfire season, outdoor air quality can deteriorate rapidly. Keep windows closed, upgrade to MERV 13 filters, consider a whole-home air purification system, and change filters more frequently.

Energy Efficiency for Historic Pasadena Homes

Pasadena's historic Craftsman and Victorian homes present unique HVAC challenges. Consider ductless mini-split systems, seal leaky windows and doors, add attic insulation, and zone your HVAC system for more efficient operation.

What size AC unit is best for Pasadena homes?

Do you offer special services for Pasadena's historic homes?

What HVAC maintenance is recommended for Pasadena's climate?
For optimal performance in Pasadena's climate, we recommend bi-annual maintenance—an AC tune-up in spring before summer heat arrives and a heating system check in fall before cooler winter temperatures.
