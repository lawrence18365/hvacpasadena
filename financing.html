Simple Application Process

Getting financing for your HVAC system is quick and easy

Complete Application

Fill out our simple online application form or apply by phone. The process takes just 5-10 minutes, and you'll only need to provide basic information like your name, address, contact details, and income.

Frequently Asked Questions

Will applying for financing affect my credit score?
No, our initial application process uses a "soft pull" credit check, which does not impact your credit score. This allows you to see what financing options you qualify for without any negative effects. A "hard pull" is only performed if you decide to proceed with a specific financing offer.
How quickly can I be approved for financing?
Most applicants receive a decision within minutes of completing the application. Once approved, you can proceed with scheduling your HVAC installation or service immediately. Our streamlined process allows us to start work on your project as soon as the next business day in many cases.
Is there a minimum credit score required?
While our primary financing options work best for credit scores of 640 and above, we have specialized financing programs for a wide range of credit situations. We believe everyone deserves a comfortable home, and we work with multiple lenders to find solutions for almost any credit profile.
Can I pay off my financing early?
Yes, all of our financing plans allow for early payoff with no prepayment penalties. This gives you the flexibility to pay off your balance ahead of schedule if your financial situation improves, potentially saving on interest charges (except on the 0% interest plan, which is already interest-free).
What documentation do I need to apply?
Our basic application requires only your contact information and basic financial details. For final approval, you may need to provide proof of identity (driver's license), proof of income (pay stubs, tax returns), and proof of residence (utility bill). Our financing team will guide you through any additional documentation needed.
