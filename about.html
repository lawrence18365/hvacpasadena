About HVAC Pasadena

Pasadena's trusted HVAC provider since 2010. Family-owned, customer-focused, and committed to excellence in every service we provide.

Our Story

HVAC Pasadena was founded in 2010 by Michael and Sarah Rodriguez, a husband and wife team with over 30 years of combined experience in the HVAC industry. What started as a small, family-operated business with just two service trucks has grown into one of the most trusted HVAC companies in the Pasadena area.

After working for large HVAC corporations, Michael and Sarah realized that many companies prioritized sales over service. They envisioned creating a company that put customers first, focused on quality workmanship, and provided honest recommendations without high-pressure sales tactics.

Today, HVAC Pasadena employs a team of 15 dedicated professionals who share our founding values. We've serviced over 7,500 homes and businesses throughout Pasadena and surrounding communities, building a reputation for reliability, technical expertise, and exceptional customer service.

As a locally owned and operated company, we take pride in serving our neighbors and contributing to the community we call home. Our commitment to excellence has earned us numerous industry certifications and a loyal customer base that continues to grow through referrals and word-of-mouth recommendations.

Community Involvement

Our commitment to giving back to the Pasadena community

Comfort for Community Program

Our signature initiative provides free HVAC services to low-income families, seniors, and veterans in need. Each year, we select families to receive free repairs, maintenance, or system replacements.

We partner with Pasadena schools to provide HVAC education, career guidance, and internship opportunities for students interested in the trades. We also sponsor athletic programs and educational initiatives.
