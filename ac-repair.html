Signs Your AC Needs Repair

Don't ignore these warning signs that indicate your air conditioner needs professional attention

AC Not Cooling Properly

If your AC is running but not cooling your home, it could indicate refrigerant leaks, compressor issues, or airflow problems.

Unusual Noises

Grinding, squealing, or banging sounds from your AC unit often indicate mechanical problems that require immediate repair.

Water Leaking or Pooling

Water around your indoor unit may indicate a clogged condensate drain line or a refrigerant leak that needs professional attention.

Skyrocketing Energy Bills

A sudden increase in your energy bills often means your AC is working harder than it should due to an underlying problem.

Weak or Limited Airflow

Poor airflow from your vents could indicate duct issues, a failing blower motor, or a clogged air filter.

Frequent Cycling

If your AC turns on and off repeatedly, it could be improperly sized, have electrical issues, or a malfunctioning thermostat.

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about AC repair in Pasadena

How quickly can you repair my AC in Pasadena?
For emergency AC repairs in Pasadena, we offer a 60-minute response time. Most repairs are completed the same day, as our service vehicles are fully stocked with common parts. Our goal is to restore your comfort as quickly as possible.
How much does AC repair cost in Pasadena?
AC repair costs vary depending on the specific issue, parts required, and the age and model of your system. Simple repairs like capacitor or contactor replacements typically range from $150-$400, while more complex repairs like compressor issues may cost $500-$1,800. We always provide a clear, written estimate before beginning any work.
Do you repair all AC brands and models?
Yes, our technicians are trained to repair all air conditioning brands and models, including Carrier, Trane, Lennox, Rheem, Goodman, York, and more. We service central air systems, ductless mini-splits, heat pumps, and packaged units.
Is it better to repair or replace my AC?
This depends on several factors: the age of your unit, repair costs vs. replacement costs, and energy efficiency. Generally, if your AC is over 10 years old and requires a major repair (costing more than 50% of a new unit), replacement is often more economical. We'll provide honest advice about your options so you can make an informed decision.
Do you offer any warranties on AC repairs?
Yes, all our AC repairs come with a 1-year warranty on parts and labor. This means if the same issue occurs within a year of our repair, we'll fix it at no additional cost to you. We also offer extended warranty options for additional peace of mind.
Can you help with AC preventative maintenance?
Absolutely! We offer comprehensive AC maintenance plans to help prevent breakdowns and extend the life of your system. Our maintenance includes thorough cleaning, inspection of all components, refrigerant level checks, and more. Regular maintenance can reduce repair needs by up to 85%.
