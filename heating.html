  • No Heat or Insufficient Heating: Troubleshooting and repairing when your furnace isn't producing heat

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about heating services

How often should I have my furnace serviced?
We recommend having your furnace professionally serviced once a year, ideally in the fall before the heating season begins. Regular maintenance helps prevent breakdowns, extends system life, maintains efficiency, and keeps your warranty valid. For homes with high usage or residents with respiratory issues, twice-yearly maintenance may be beneficial.
How long do furnaces typically last?
With proper maintenance, a well-made furnace can last 15-20 years. However, if your furnace is over 15 years old and requiring frequent repairs, it may be more cost-effective to replace it with a new, more energy-efficient model. Older furnaces typically operate at 65-70% efficiency, while new high-efficiency models can achieve up to 98% efficiency, significantly reducing your energy bills.
What are signs that my furnace needs repair?
Common signs include unusual noises (banging, rattling, whistling), insufficient heat, frequent cycling on and off, increased energy bills, yellow pilot light instead of blue, and strange odors. If your furnace is showing any of these symptoms, it's best to have it inspected by a professional technician before a minor issue becomes a major problem or safety hazard.
How much does a new furnace cost?
The cost of a new furnace depends on several factors including the type, size, efficiency rating, and features. In the Pasadena area, basic furnace installations typically start around $3,000, while high-efficiency models with advanced features can range from $5,000-$8,000. We offer free in-home consultations to provide an accurate quote based on your specific needs and home requirements.
Do you offer financing for new heating systems?
Yes, we offer flexible financing options to help make your heating system upgrade more affordable. We work with reputable financing partners to provide competitive rates and terms, including no-interest plans for qualified customers. During your consultation, we'll explain all available financing options to help you find a plan that fits your budget.
How quickly can you respond to a heating emergency?
We offer 24/7 emergency heating services with a 60-minute response time guarantee for most Pasadena neighborhoods. We understand that heating failures often occur at the most inconvenient times, so our emergency team is always ready to restore your comfort as quickly as possible, day or night, weekends and holidays included.
